About

A little more about me

Motivation

My interest in programming began at a young age, during high school. The curriculum allowed me to focus on C++ programming, primarily for solving logical problems and algorithms. I was often ahead of the class, always looking for the most efficient ways to tackle complex exercises.

This early enthusiasm for C++, combined with my love for drawing and video games, naturally led me toward the gaming industry.

Journey

Although I was determined to pursue a career in Game Development, the opportunities available in Romania were limited. I knew I would need to make some sacrifices to follow my dream path. After thorough research, I discovered that the program offered by Digital Arts and Entertainment in Belgium was the right fit for me.

While I was initially drawn to the artistic side of game development as well, I ultimately chose to specialize in Gameplay Programming.

Hobbies

I consider myself someone who rarely gets bored. In my free time, aside from working on personal indie game projects, I enjoy reading, drawing, watching anime, and playing video games. I also stay fairly active by going to the gym, practicing yoga or pilates, and going on nature walks — they’re my cup of tea.
