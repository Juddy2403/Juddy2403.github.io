Motivation

My interest in programming started at a rather young age, while I was in high school. My curriculum enabled me to focus on C++ programming, mainly used for solving logical problems and algorithms. I was always ahead of class, looking forward for the most efficient ways of solving the most complicated exercises.

That enthusiasm and interest I started developing for C++, combined with my love for drawing and video games directed me towards the game industry.

My initial desire was to combine two of my hobbies that I loved: art and programming. I thought the gaming industry was the best place for me to manifest the two, though after careful consideration I decided I want to specialize in one of the two rather than learn both at a basic level. I decided to choose programming, namely in the context of Game Development.

Journey

I began learning C++ at 14, during high school, and quickly grasped its logical foundations. This led me to participate in several local C++ competitions.
